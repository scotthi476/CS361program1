This quiz program is designed to learn Japanese vocabulary.
The quiz format is a multiple-choice format.  You are provided a Japanese word
written in Japanese and 4 options in English.  You are going to select a correct English
option for the given Japanese word.  There are 3 levels for this quiz, the beginner level,
the intermediate level, and the advanced level. At the beginner level, a Japanese word is
given in Hiragana.  At the intermediate level, Japanese word is given in both Hiragana
and Katakana.  At the advanced level, the Japanese word includes Kanji characters.
Once you enter your selection and enter, then the correct answer will be displayed.
There will be 20 questions for each level, and at the end of the quiz set,
your total score will be displayed.
